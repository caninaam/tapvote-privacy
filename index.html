TapVote — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

TapVote ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we use it.

1. Data We Collect

  • Email address — used for account creation and authentication.
  • Instagram username — used to verify photo ownership before submission.
  • Push notification token — used to send you weekly results and competition updates.
  • Submitted photos — Instagram post URLs submitted voluntarily by users for the weekly competition.
  • Votes — records of taps cast on photos (linked to your user ID).
  • Country — selected by the user for country-level rankings.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • To verify that submitted photos belong to you.
  • To display weekly rankings and results.
  • To send push notifications about competition results.
  • To prevent duplicate votes and submissions.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and database services. Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Expo — push notification delivery. Expo Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account exists. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

5. Children

TapVote is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at the email below.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions: contact@tapvote.app
