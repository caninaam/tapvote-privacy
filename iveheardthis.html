IveHeardThis — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

IveHeardThis ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we use it.

1. Data We Collect

IveHeardThis does not create user accounts and does not store any personal data on external servers. All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Username — a display name you choose on first launch, stored locally on your device.
  • Quiz statistics — your score, streaks, and answers per category, stored locally on your device.
  • App preferences — your selected language, stored locally on your device.

2. AI-Generated Content (Anthropic Claude API)

Quiz questions are generated on demand using the Anthropic Claude API. Each request includes only:

  • The selected content category (literature, cinema, music, manga)
  • The selected sub-domain and app language
  • A list of recently used answer titles to avoid repetition

No personal information is included in these requests. Anthropic's privacy policy applies: anthropic.com/privacy.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

IveHeardThis displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve relevant ads, including device identifiers, IP address, and general location. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except as described above for the Anthropic Claude API and Google AdMob.

5. Data Retention

All app data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data.

6. Children

IveHeardThis is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
